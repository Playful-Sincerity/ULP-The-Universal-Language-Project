# Prosody Symbols — New Punctuation Beyond ! and ?

Create typeable symbols representing base prosody patterns. Two approaches:

1. **Semantic prosody** — symbols for the meaning of the tone (curious, assertive, tentative). More translatable.
2. **Linguistic prosody** — symbols for tone modulation (rising, falling, staccato). More precise.

Placement options: after sentences, within words, above text (like diacritics), or as index notation. Simpler than per-syllable tones (cf. Mandarin pinyin) — use semantic groupings where one symbol covers phrase-level prosody.
