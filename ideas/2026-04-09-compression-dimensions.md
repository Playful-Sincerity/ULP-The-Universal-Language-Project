# Dimensions as Modes of Compression

From play session 2026-04-09. These are candidate theoretical advances for ULP's dimensional ladder.

## Core Formulations

### 1. Points Are Realities
A point at dimension N fully specifies a complete (N-1)-dimensional configuration. A point in 4D spacetime IS a complete 3D spatial arrangement. This is mathematically true (standard physics) but arrived at from a semiotic direction — through thinking about how signals encode meaning, not through Minkowski spacetime. The convergence of paths is meaningful.

### 2. Each Dimension = Universe Finding a New Way to Converge
Dimensions aren't containers. They're modes of convergence. The universe has one drive (convergence/compression) and each dimension is a new medium through which it performs that drive:
- Gravity compresses space (spatial convergence)
- Time compresses change (temporal convergence)
- Information compresses patterns (representational convergence)
- Consciousness compresses the act of compression itself (meta-convergence)

### 3. Information Is Gravity That Works at a Distance
Why is information a higher dimension than space? Because it breaks the locality constraint. Gravity only works on things that are near. Information can compress things that are anywhere, anywhen — but only as representation. DNA in a cell compresses the shape of a protein that doesn't exist yet. A brain in SF compresses the state of a park it hasn't visited. Simulation breaks locality. Within a simulation, you CAN time travel and teleport.

### 4. Compression = Representation (Same Act)
You can't compress without creating a representation. Every representation is inherently a compression. These aren't sequential — they're the same act. DNA doesn't represent proteins as an extra step. DNA IS the spatial universe compressing itself through a new channel.

### 5. The 1111 vs 1010101 Problem
Both contain 4 units of "on." But:
- 1111 = magnitude without internal structure (a thing with mass, a rock)
- 1010101 = magnitude WITH internal structure (a thing with pattern/dimension)
The alternation in 1010101 creates an internal dimension — addressable positions, distinguishable parts — that 1111 doesn't have. And perceiving that alternation requires operating from the dimension above the signal.

### 6. The Observer Requirement
"101 represents separation because we've assumed a simulation is going and representing the thing." Meaning IS the dimensional gap between signal and perceiver. A ULP decoder must operate at least one dimension above the signal it's reading. Always.

### 7. Multiple-Within vs Multiple-Of
You can have multiple 2D shapes inside 2D space. That doesn't require 3D. But to have multiple INSTANCES of 2D space itself — multiple separate planes — you need 3D. The higher dimension is needed for multiplicity of the CONTAINER, not multiplicity of objects within it.

### 8. Why Is 3D→4D Specifically "Change"?
Time is what you get when a single 3D position needs to hold more than one value. The moment a point needs to be "this AND then that," you need an axis to index those different states. Time adds new STATES for existing positions (unlike spatial dimensions which add new positions). That's a qualitative shift — the first self-referential dimension.
