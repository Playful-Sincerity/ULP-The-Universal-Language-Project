## 5. Conceptual Shifts Visible in the Arc

### Shift 1: 7 unnamed primitives → named dimensional hierarchy (Oct 2020 → Jan 2023)

October 29, 2020: 7 primitives numbered 0–6, all unnamed. By January 2023, the `语言` log shows two-digit encoding (42=time, 250=time, 125=temporal object) with the primitives being reasoned about systematically. The shift is from intuitive count to structured encoding.

### Shift 2: Grammar as patch vs. 2D syntax as substrate (Dec 2022)

> "grammar is mostly useful for homophone distinction. On a two dimensional plan with completely distinction between morphemes, grammar is not as necessary." (12/27/2022)

First explicit argument that 2D layout renders grammar redundant — grammar is a workaround for the ambiguity of linear text. This reframes the writing system question: not "how do we add grammar?" but "how do we build a system that doesn't need it?"

### Shift 3: Sems coined and binary encoding begins (Jan 25, 2023)

The abbreviated term "Sems" appears alongside early two-digit codes (42=time, 250=time). The system shifts from qualitative description to encoding — each concept gets a number, numbers compose. The `0`, `∞`, and `*` modifiers appear as dimensional scaling operators.

### Shift 4: Dimensional ordering of mind (Feb 2023)

> "DNA is 1d, Feeling is 2d? Thought 3d" (17/2/2023)

The dimension ladder is applied to biology and cognition: DNA as 1D storage, emotion as 2D, thought as 3D. This grounds the abstract dimensional system in observable structure and opens the path toward consciousness dimensions (7, 8, 9+).

### Shift 5: The consciousness dimension map (Jun 2024)

> "5 is time, 6 is like recording, 7 is like simulation, 8 is like the awareness of the simulation... 9 could be social stuff, 10 then could be ideals" (6/6/2024)

The most complete single-note mapping of the higher-dimensional ladder. Consciousness gets tier 8, subconscious tier 7, social tier 9. This is the first time the ladder extends coherently through consciousness into social and ideal dimensions.

### Shift 6: Formal nesting rule stated (Feb 2025)

> "When a dimension 'n' is nested within a dimension 'm' (m > n), the meaning of the combination 'mn' is the set of all possible instances of dimension 'n' within the context defined by dimension 'm'" (18/2/25)

The nesting operation becomes definitional. A two-digit code `mn` is no longer just a label — it has a compositional semantics. This is the move from notation to algebra.

### Shift 7: Tier-justification criterion (Jan 2026)

> "A new tier becomes meaningful when relations at the prior tier can be stabilized into a unit that behaves like a 'thing' at the next tier. A tier is justified only if it is the smallest closure over the previous tier that produces a new kind of stable object/constraint that you cannot express as a simple combination below." (23/1/2026)

This is the arrival of a formal axiom for the dimensional ladder — not "I feel like this deserves its own tier" but a structural criterion. Each tier earns its existence by producing a new kind of stable object. The entry immediately adjacent: "dynamics produces patterns; some patterns become compressible/storable invariants; that's information." — which gives an example instantiation of the criterion.
